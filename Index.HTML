Game Instructions

You are a medieval knight who has just slain his first dragon. Now, before you meet your damsel in distress, you wish to collect her a beautiful bouquet of roses. However, beware the poisonous thorns in the rose bush! Should you prick your finger, you shall never see your beautiful Dame again!

Objective:

Try to pick as many roses as possible and avoid the thorns in the limited amount of time you have. Should you pick a thorn, your game shall end and the number of roses you have collected shall be recorded
